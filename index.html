My Story

Ever seen a super-hero movie with a bad ending ?

Like the one where the bad guys win ?

Yeah neither have I ...

But lets rewind a bit here


Let me introduce myself...
I am the one and only -Captain Levi

... Actually that's my super-hero name, my real name is Aayush Khanna.

Fall '17 ...

It all started when I was bitten by a radio-active spider during my Web Technologies lecture ...

... and for the past year and a half I've grown a passion for everything web-related!

At first, my senses became super responsive to every detail, every annoying bug, every gorgeous design ...

... Over time my new found powers made me appreciate the web world around me even more !

USC - Wireless Devices and Systems Group

Los Angeles

... eager to test my superpowers, I bootstrapped the markup and weaved it with jQuery to saved the day for the WIDES !

And I knew - this was just the beginning !
USC Institute for Creative Technologies

Spring '18

Impressed by my styles, I got invited to join The Avengers ICT!

... where I learnt to harness the power of the React-or with imaculate Sass !

SupplyFrame HQ

... Summer '18

Confident and Prideful of my superpowers, I was chosen to work with the elite to protect the city of hackaday ...
Here I faced the ultimate challenge, my biggest nemesis - The Back-End!

... With the entire HQ relying on me, I learnt to navigate the express voyager over the node, got better at weaving the web, and managed to secure the end-point!

Fall '18 ...

caught in-between the stuggles of my secret identity as a grad student, I failed multiple times ...

But I continued to fight the swarm of bugs on the git, made major commits and revamped the interface !

Most importantly, I leart how important it is to ask for help when in doubt!

Spring '19 ...

I bid goodbye to my alies at the HQ and parts ways

"he's the hero hackaday deserves, but not the one it needs right now ... because he can take it, because he's not a hero..."

"... he's a silent guardian, a watchful protector ..."
